Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the conversion?

Accuracy depends on PDF quality. Clean, text-based PDFs typically achieve 95%+ accuracy. Our QA flags help you identify areas that need verification. All critical parameters should be manually reviewed before use.

What file formats can I export to?

We support exports to DOCX, Excel (for tabular data), CSV, and structured JSON. You can choose the format that best fits your laboratory information management system or workflow.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest. Files are processed in isolated environments and permanently deleted after 24 hours. We never train models on your proprietary protocols.
